The magic explained

How does "datafree" actually work?

MoyaApp is zero-rated on MTN, Vodacom, Cell C and Telkom. Text messages don't count toward your data bundle — they're carried on a separate channel. When you're on Wi-Fi or have data, all features open up. When data runs out, texts keep going.
