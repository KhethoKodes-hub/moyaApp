Yes — download is free and texting remains free when your data runs out on supported networks. No hidden costs.

You can download it anywhere, but datafree texts only work on SA networks (MTN, Vodacom, Cell C, Telkom).

First download is ~50 MB. After that, texts use zero data when out of bundle; photos and calls use minimal data.
