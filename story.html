Our story

From a simple idea to a super app keeping millions connected — even with zero data.

Cape Town, 2018
2018
Where it began

A message worth sending,
even without data

In 2018, our founders noticed that millions of South Africans had to choose between buying data and staying in touch with loved ones. We believed connectivity shouldn't be a luxury. So we built MoyaApp — the first messaging platform in the country to offer free, unlimited texts when your data runs out.

What started as a small team in Cape Town is now used by over 6.5 million people every single month, across all major South African networks.
